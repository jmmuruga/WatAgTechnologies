WATER TREATMENT SYSTEMS
Reverse Osmosis Plant(RO)

A Reverse Osmosis Plant (RO) is a water purication process which incorporates the use of a partially permeable membrane to remove unwanted molecules and larger particles from drinking water. Reverse osmosis is used to deliver exceptionally clean water for drinking purposes as well as large scale industrial applications.

UltraFiltration

Ultra Filtration is an eective means of reducing the silt density index of water and removing particulates that can foul reverse osmosis membranes. Ultra ltration is frequently used to pre-treat surface water, seawater and biologically treated municipal water upstream from the reverse osmosis unit.

Softener

Water Softeners work through a process called ion-exchange which eliminates calcium and magnesium from the water. When the hard water enters into the mineral tank, it ows through a bed of spherical resin beads. These plastic beads, usually made from polystyrene, are charged with a sodium ion.
